KPI Metrics Metric Status
Comments: Membership is 100 above year-end 2025.

Comments: US is up 7% YTD, while International is

10%.
Metric Information: First-time candidates are unique candidates who, for 
the first time, sit for either Exam P or Exam FM and receive a record on 
their transcript.
Comments: Research revenue and expenses are

Metric Information: Ratio of revenue supporting research over the sum of 
1) staff cost net of time spent on PD/Engagement activities plus 2) direct 
costs of research (e.g., contractor spending).
Comments: The first of two satisfaction surveys 
will take place in the summer of 2026.
Metric Information: Average satisfaction scores for: “Thinking of your 
experiences over the past 12 months, how would you rate your 
satisfaction with the SOA overall?”, conducted over two candidate 
surveys.
Comments: US first-time candidate diversity has 
observed strong increases in Mar.-May after a slower 
start in 2026 when compared to 2025.

from the US who don't identify as either Asian or Caucasian, divided by 
the total US First-time Candidates that provide a race/ethnicity.

satisfaction).
