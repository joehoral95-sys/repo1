# Brief: _example-quarterly

This is the shipped worked example (and the CI golden deck). It shows what a
finished brief + spec look like – copy its shape for real decks.

- **Audience:** SOA Board of Directors – strategic, time-poor, numbers-fluent.
  They want the decision framed, not the raw data.
- **Intent:** Reassure on the membership trend; get sign-off on the 4%
  exam-fee proposal.
- **Date / occasion:** July 2026 board meeting, 10-minute slot.
- **Candidate insights (from the insight pass):**
  1. Longest membership growth streak since 2019 – lead with strength.
  2. The exam dip is *concentrated in prelims*; fellowship demand is stable – 
     it's a pipeline issue, not a retention problem. (This reframes the bad
     news and was the sender's buried lede.)
  3. Two-thirds of candidates now arrive via universities – the calendar-shift
     explanation for the prelim dip becomes plausible and checkable.
  4. Fee raise still leaves SOA below peer bodies – kills the main objection.
